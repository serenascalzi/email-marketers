Email Marketers: The ABCs of Writing Effective Subject Lines

Writing effective subject lines is a challenge faced by many Digital Marketing professionals. As a Digital Marketing Manager for one of the largest credit card issuers in the country, I have experienced this challenge firsthand.

In the blog post 6 Shocking Myths About Subject Lines, Chad White of Litmus addresses common myths surrounding email subject lines. White dispels myths about the effectiveness of mysterious and misleading subject lines whose sole goal is to generate opens. Instead, White suggests writing clear and descriptive subject lines targeted to audiences that are likely to click and convert.

Austin Distel

In the blog post 6 Ways to Get Your Subject Lines Noticed, Jomel Alos of PureB2B offers tips for writing attention grabbing email subject lines. Alos suggests creating short subject lines that include numbers, symbols, and emojis. According to Alos, while subject lines with personalization and lists are effective, those with urgency are not.

These blog posts provide the basis for the ABCs of writing effective subject lines.

  • Be Accurate – Write straightforward subject lines that describe your email.
  • Be Brief – Write short subject lines that incorporate numbers and emojis.
  • Be Customer-centric – Write personalized subject lines geared toward your target audience.

Remember these ABCs to meet the challenge of writing effective subject lines head on!
